About Us

Formsy is a global professional services platform designed to simplify business registration, compliance, and growth solutions for entrepreneurs, startups, and enterprises. We bridge the gap between innovative ideas and the legal structure required to scale them, ensuring that every founder can focus on building while we manage the complexities of paperwork.

Our core services include company incorporation, startup registration, compliance management, tax filings, licenses, and intellectual property protection such as trademarks and copyrights. By combining technology with expert-driven support, Formsy delivers fast, transparent, and reliable solutions that help businesses establish a strong legal identity and stay growth-ready.

We are committed to empowering the startup ecosystem worldwide by providing accessible, scalable, and hassle-free services tailored to the unique needs of founders and growing enterprises. Whether it's launching a new venture, protecting a brand, or managing ongoing compliance, Formsy ensures clarity, accuracy, and trust at every step.
